# Operator guide — GIN

Portal: no confirmed dedicated self-service e-procurement portal --
notices/texts are published via ARMP (Autorité de Régulation des
Marchés Publics, armpguinee.org) and the Ministère de l'Économie et des
Finances (mef.gov.gn); the Primature's own "DOCS" Lois/Décrets listing
pages returned no visible content to this iteration's static fetch, an
honest gap. Regulated by ARMP (independent regulator, Code des Marchés
Publics Article 16) and DGCMP (Direction Générale du Contrôle des
Marchés Publics, a priori/a posteriori control, per the Ministère de
l'Économie et des Finances' own directorate listing) under the Code des
Marchés Publics (décret signed 17 December 2019, implementing Loi
L/2012/020/CNT du 11 octobre 2012 as amended by Loi L/2018/028/AN du 05
juillet 2018). Entity: RCCM (OHADA AUDCG, performed by the Tribunal de
Commerce de Conakry) + NIFP tax record (Direction Générale des Impôts).
Engagements declaring a prior ARMP pecuniary sanction also clear the
Code's own Article 159 statutory 1%-2% percentage-range gate before a
filing can proceed. Human-gated filing only.
